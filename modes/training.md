# Mode: Training — Summer School & Workshop Evaluation

**Trigger:** User says "evaluate this workshop", "is this summer school worth it", or `/academic-ops training`

## Workflow

1. **Read context:**
   - `cv.md` — user's CV
   - `config/profile.yml` — field, level, career stage
   - Position URL or description

2. **Extract details:**
   - Name, dates, location, cost
   - Organizing institution/society
   - Topics covered, speakers/lecturers
   - Application requirements, acceptance rate (if known)
   - Funding/travel grants available
   - Certificate or credit offered

3. **Score on 6 dimensions:**

| Dimension | Weight | Measures |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Topic Relevance | 25% | How closely does the content match your research needs? |
| Speaker Quality | 20% | Are the lecturers leaders in the field? Would you benefit from meeting them? |
| Network Value | 20% | Will other participants be valuable contacts? (PhD students, postdocs in your field) |
| Career Signal | 15% | Does attending signal expertise or commitment in your field? |
| Cost-Benefit | 10% | Is the cost (time + money + travel) justified by the value? |
| Practical Skills | 10% | Will you learn concrete techniques you can apply immediately? |

4. **Verdict:**
   - Score ≥ 4.0 → "**Attend.** [specific reasons]"
   - Score 3.0–3.9 → "**Consider if [condition].** [trade-offs]"
   - Score < 3.0 → "**Skip.** [better alternatives]"

5. **Suggest alternatives** if score is low — similar events that might be a better fit.
